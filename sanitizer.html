How to Make a Sanitizer


Hand sanitizers play a huge role in preventing the spread of germs through hands. They are also a good alternative when you don't have soap and water. COVID-19 is an envelope virus, and an effective santizer can inactivate the envelope, which makes the virus ineffective.



The Procedure

1. Take 100 gram neem leaves and 20 basil leaves and put them in a cup.
2. Add water (according to the quantity of sanitizer you want to make)
3. Boil it for a couple of minutes
4. After boiled, add fresh Aloe Vera gel
5. Boil it again for a couple of minutes
6. Add 10 gram alum
7. Boil it once more for a couple of minutes
8. Keep it in the refrigerator for 30 minutes. Your sanitizer is now done.
